
These are the codes for my final year project for my university course, im currently still doing it.

its a bus tracking app to track the locations of campus busses around university malaya

- UMBusTracker : bus tracking app installed on a device permanently on the bus, send back GPS coordinates 
  and other info to the server

- UMBusTracking : app to download the locations of busses from server and display on a googlemaps, also shows 
  other infos to the user(stops/stations location, total number of busses along a route, etc)
